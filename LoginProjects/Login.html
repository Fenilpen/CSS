Here’s a quick breakdown of **Flexbox vs. Grid**: - **Flexbox** is **one-dimensional**, meaning it works along a single axis—either **row** or **column**. It’s great for aligning items dynamically and handling responsive layouts where elements adjust based on content size. - **Grid** is **two-dimensional**, meaning it lets you control both **rows and columns** simultaneously. It’s perfect for complex layouts where you need precise placement of elements. Think of **Flexbox** as arranging items **in a line**, while **Grid** is like setting up a **spreadsheet** where you define rows and columns
