Here are a few key points: - **Clarity & Simplicity**: Keep labels clear and intuitive so users can easily find what they need. - **Responsive Design**: Ensure it adapts well to different screen sizes, especially for mobile users. - **Sticky or Fixed Nav**: If needed, keep the navbar visible while scrolling for better accessibility. - **Dropdowns & Icons**: Use them wisely—too many can overwhelm users, while too few may limit navigation. - **CTA Placement**: If there’s an important action (like signing up), position it prominently for visibility. - **Accessibility & Contrast**: Ensure text is readable and the navigation is usable for everyone.
