Key Formulas & Concepts: - grid-template-columns: repeat(n, size) → Defines the number of columns (`n`) and their size. -grid-template-rows: repeat(n, size) → Defines the number of rows (`n`) and their size. - grid-gap: size → Sets spacing between grid items. - grid-column` & `grid-row` → Defines placement for individual elements. **Flexbox (Flexible Box Layout)** - Designed for **one-dimensional layouts** (either a row or a column). - Best for **aligning** and **distributing space** among items within a container. - Items can **stretch, shrink, and align** dynamically based on available space. ### **Grid (CSS Grid Layout)** - Designed for **two-dimensional layouts** (rows and columns). - Best for **structuring** a full-page or complex component-based designs. - Allows precise **placement** of elements in a grid-like structure.
